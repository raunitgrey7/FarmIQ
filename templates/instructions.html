Welcome to KisaanSaathi 🌾

This is your one-stop platform for crop recommendations, soil type prediction, and more.

How to Use KisaanSaathi

  1. Go to Crop tab and enter soil data to get crop advice.
  2. Go to Disease tab and upload a photo of your plant leaves.
  3. Check Weather tab for local forecast before sowing.
  4. Visit Issues to report or understand common crop problems.
